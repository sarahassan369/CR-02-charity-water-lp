Our Story

Charity: Water’s story begins with a challenge too vast to ignore—millions of people around the world living without access to clean water. Founded on the belief that the global water crisis can be solved through the power of community, we partner with local organizations to build sustainable water projects that transform lives.

Every drop counts, and every community deserves safe, clean water. We are committed to transparency and accountability, showing you exactly how every dollar you donate makes an impact—from the moment your gift is received to when fresh water flows in a community. Our projects are built to last, designed for sustainability to ensure clean water access for generations to come. Whether it’s wells or rainwater harvesting systems, we focus on solutions that stand the test of time.

This mission is about more than just water — it’s about giving people time, health, dignity, and opportunity. When a community gains access to clean water, everything changes. Our story is still being written, and we're inviting you to be a part of it.

Real Impact: Meet Daniel

Before clean water came to his village in Uganda, Daniel, age 10, often missed school to help his mother carry water from a muddy pond miles away. The water smelled bad, and more often than not, someone in the family was sick from drinking it. When a Charity: Water project funded a drilled well near their home, everything changed. Daniel now walks just minutes to get clean, safe water. He’s back in school full time, learning math and dreaming of becoming a teacher. His mother says, “We used to pray for water. Now, we give thanks every day.” Your support doesn’t just bring water — it brings opportunity, dignity, and hope.
